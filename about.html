About me

I'm Lomi — a product designer based in Seoul, South Korea. I design digital products that feel a little easier, a little more enjoyable, and a little more human.

What I do

I work across the full product design process — from research and data analysis to interaction design and final UI. I care about the details that make an experience feel considered: clear information architecture, thoughtful micro-interactions, and copy that actually helps.

Lately, I've been exploring the space between design and development — using AI tools like Claude Code to prototype directly from Figma designs, and building reusable skills and agents that help designers work more independently.
